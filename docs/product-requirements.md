NeuralHandoff — Product Requirements Document (PRD)
1. Product Overview
NeuralHandoff is a multi-tenant Enterprise Knowledge Intelligence Platform that enables organizations to securely store, search, retrieve and interact with internal knowledge using AI.

2. Target Customers
Customer Support Teams
Engineering Teams
HR & Onboarding Teams
Compliance & Legal Teams

Knowledge scattered across tools
Slow search & onboarding

Loss of institutional knowledge
Security risks with unmanaged access
